Our Story

Our story began in 2020 when businesses all around the world started to shut due to the Coronavirus pandemic, a bunch of working professionals and freelancers joined hands to start a new journey together to help small-scale and upcoming businesses reap the benefits of digital marketing.

At The Social Tadka, we have always believed in the importance of building a great, enduring relationship with our partners, striking a balance between profitability and a social conscience.

It started with two individuals, our co-founders, understanding that our future is inextricably tied to the future of our respective clients and their businesses. We nurtured innumerable brand identities while working in two different organizations, and built a PAN India network of support to create an authentic yet quirky way to manage brands and businesses: one that is sustainable, transparent, and good for both the parties involved.

As the COVID-19 pandemic has brought the world to a standstill, we have been working tirelessly to help businesses adapt to the digital market and find innovative solutions in how we build and operate our clients’ brand images, while making sure to keep our services financially reasonable.

Stitching all these efforts together, we are dedicated to making our partners proud. As it has been from the beginning, our purpose goes far beyond profit. We believe in the pursuit of doing good.
